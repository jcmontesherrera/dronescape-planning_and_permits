## Landholder template

- [ ] _**How to use this template:** Duplicate this file and rename it `NN-[ORIGIN]-[DEST]-Access-applications.md` (e.g. `03-BRK-DAR-Access-applications.md`). Fill in trip details below, then for each site create a card with: **Organisation / owner** · **Tenure** (national park, nature reserve, SCA, Crown Land, private, other) · **Jurisdiction** (LGA name or Unincorporated Area) · **Plot IDs** (specific TERN site IDs) · **Who to address** (role or name if known) · **Channel** (email, phone, or both) · **Grouping** (e.g. shared regional inbox). Move cards across columns as the approval workflow progresses._
- [ ] _**Trip:** [TRIP-ID] · **Route:** [ORIGIN] → [DESTINATION] · **Field dates:** [START DATE] – [END DATE] · **Sites:** [TOTAL SITE COUNT] plots across [N] properties_


## Properties

- [ ] _Card moves here once the landholder/authority has been identified and site jurisdiction confirmed. Fill in land tenure, contact details, and all plot IDs before moving to **To Contact**._


## To Contact

- [ ] _Card moves here once landholder details are complete and you are ready to draft outreach. Use the email template in `templates/email-template-nationalpark.md` as a base, attach maps, and tailor to the specific authority type (NPWS, Crown Lands, Council, private)._


## Documents & Maps

- [ ] _Card moves here once contact has replied with required forms, or once you begin preparing maps and the Area Manager Approval form for a site. Track form status inline: **received · returned · awaiting approval**._


## Awaiting Response

- [ ] _Card moves here once email and all attachments have been sent and you are waiting on approval. Note the date sent and expected response window._


## Access Confirmed

- [x] _Card moves here once approval is received. Update with: **Ranger / contact name** · **Phone** · **Gate / access notes** · **Any conditions or restrictions** · **Expiry date of permission** (if applicable)._
